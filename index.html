How do I store and take care of my shoes?

I learned my lesson when I was in the Philippines. I thought they will not get ruined if I keep them in the box and take them out when I'm about to wear them. However, I learned the hard way. My most loved shoes which I only wore once, started peeling. Straps were also weak. I googled and found out that the best way to take care of them is just to leave them out and ust dust them when you need to use them.

How do I choose my shoes?

Fashion is not my only concern. When I start looking for a new pair, I start with the price. If it is too pricey, then I start looking for coupons to use. If after finding coupons, it is still not something I want to spend, then I let it go. One thing I am usually doing is buying my winter shoes during spring or summer and buying my summer shoes during winter. This way, they are definitely in clearance and can still use multiple coupons to make them cheaper.
